Welcome to the "Diseases in SciO Bio Events" Page!

Consider this as a parting gift from a senior who just wants people to enjoy SciO. Science Olympiad was (and still is) such a blessing to me. It got me interested in pursuing biological sciences and research in the public health industry. The event that started this interest? Cell Biology (what an event *wipes tear*). Hope they bring that event back. Anyways, Microbe Mission ("the new Cell Bio"), Disease Detectives, and Anatomy and Phys definitely have quite a few diseases, so explore and browse!
